VIDEO EDITING







I used to edit on a variety of programs and apps since 2018. I started on Powerdirector App, then Cute Cut Pro, Kinemaster, and now, Sony Vegas.

The moment I knew that I love editing was when I discovered to myself that I enjoy editing videos.
